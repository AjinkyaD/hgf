How Queries Are Handled

Hospital Guide Foundation (HGF) will revert with suggestions within 72 hours of getting the query, provided Patient/Attendant has responded to the initial email/call from HGF with relevant details promptly.

  • Patient/Attendant sends a query.
  • After receiving the query, Hospital Guide Foundation (HGF) will email/call Patient/Attendant(P/A) to get more relevant details.
  • HGF will study & evaluate the request.
  • HGF will revert with suggestions & contact details within 72 hours of getting the query, provided P/A has responded to the initial email/call with the relevant details promptly.
  • P/A will seek services from the service providers (Doctors, hospitals) whose reference HGF has given.
  • P/A will send feedback on the services availed form the references given by HGF and feedback on the services of HGF.
